The day of

Here's what to expect.

1

Arrive 15 min early

Roll in 15 minutes before your booking to check in, drop off the cake, and get shoes handed out. We'll have your lanes ready.

2

We set the lanes

Bumpers on, names in the scoreboard, balls in the right weights. Our lane server will greet your party and run food & drinks.

3

Bowl + eat + cake

Two hours of bowling flies by. Pizza hits the lanes about 45 minutes in. Cake whenever you want — we'll clear space.

4

Arcade time

Lots of kids love running to the arcade area between frames. Bring some extra cash for tokens or we can add tokens to your package.

5

Goodie bags (on you)

Bring your own goodie bags — we don't sell pre-packaged ones, but you're welcome to distribute whatever you bring.

6

We clean up

When the party's over, grab the leftovers and go. We'll handle the tables, trash, and pin-setters. Promise.

Birthday FAQ

Parent questions, answered.

How early should I book?

Weekends book out 2–4 weeks ahead, especially Saturdays. Weekday afternoons are easier to grab last-minute. Summer and December fill up fast — book early.

Can we bring our own cake and decorations?

Yes to cake, cupcakes, balloons, banners, and goodie bags. No outside food beyond the cake, please — our kitchens cover everything else.

Do you have bumpers and lighter balls?

Absolutely. Every lane has bumpers that can go up or down on demand, and we have balls in every weight from 6 lbs on up for smaller kids.

What about non-bowlers?

No problem. Non-bowlers can hang at the lane, hit the arcade, grab food, or shoot pool (over-21 in the bar area). You only pay for actual bowlers in lane packages.

Can I add more pizzas or food to the package?

Yes — extra pizzas, wing trays, tenders, salad platters, and catering from Kim's Noodle House can all be added. Ask when you book.

Do you require a deposit?

A deposit holds your lanes. Amount depends on the package. Theresa will walk you through it when you call to book.

Is the birthday kid's bowling free?

Lane packages cover up to 20 or 40 bowlers, so the birthday kid is included. In per-guest packages, the birthday kid is often comp'd — ask Theresa when booking.
