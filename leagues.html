Leagues & regular play

Ten-pin. Nine-pin. Specialty rules. Whatever you bowl, we host it.

Every style, every skill

Westside is built for league play.

Whether you're already in an established league or just looking to join one, Westside Lanes is equipped to support every kind of play. Our technicians handle ten-pin, nine-pin, and specialty-rule leagues alike — and our twenty modern lanes give you the room to compete.

League play runs Monday through Saturday. Substitute spots open up regularly, new teams are always welcome, and our party packages make end-of-season banquets a breeze.

What we host

Pick your lane.

A mix of competitive, social and specialty leagues for every kind of bowler.

Ten-Pin Leagues

The classic. Men's, women's, mixed and senior leagues. Weeknight slots available — ask about substitutes and open spots.

Nine-Pin Nights

Faster frames, more action, more chaos. Great for newer bowlers looking for a fun weekly night out.

Specialty Leagues

Glow bowling, cosmic nights, themed seasonal leagues and more. If your crew has a format in mind, bring it to us.

Corporate Leagues

Great for team-building. We'll set up dedicated lanes for your office crew, with food, drinks and a scoreboard of your own.

Youth & Family

Bumpers, lighter balls, and family-friendly times so the kids can roll alongside mom and dad.

Pool & Dart Leagues

Not a bowler? We also host pool and dart leagues in the 21+ bar area — weeknight action, cold drafts on tap.

How to join

New to league play?
Here's the drill.

  1. Call Theresa Tran at 504-444-8103. She's our league coordinator and she'll help you find the right night and the right format for you.
  2. Pick a league. Whether you want competitive, social, or somewhere in between, we'll match you with a team or spot.
  3. Show up and roll. We'll have your lane ready, your shoes waiting, and a dedicated server for food and drinks.

Want to form your own team? Grab 3–4 friends and we'll get you started. Party packages are available for end-of-season banquets too.

FAQ

Common league questions.

How often do leagues meet?

Most leagues meet once a week, same night, same time, for a set season (typically 16–32 weeks). Scheduling depends on the league you join — Theresa will walk you through options.

Do I have to be a good bowler to join?

Not at all. Most leagues use a handicap system so new and experienced bowlers compete fairly. We'll place you in a league that fits your skill level.

What if I can't make every week?

Substitutes are welcome, and subs are a great way to try league play without committing. Call Theresa and ask about sub lists for the nights you're free.

Can I bring my own ball and shoes?

Absolutely. Bring your own gear, or rent from us — we have house balls in every weight and shoes in every size.

Where can I see live scores and standings?

Live scores and standings are posted at livescores.computerscore.com.au. Bookmark it and check in after the night's done.

Do you host tournaments?

We do. One-day tournaments, charity fundraisers, and end-of-season shoot-outs happen regularly. Call us to ask what's on the calendar, or follow us on Facebook for announcements.
