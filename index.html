Parties & events

Where your next big night goes down.

Birthdays, graduations, weddings, office parties, fundraisers — Westside is a no-brainer venue. We book by the lane, not by the headcount, so small groups and big crews are all welcome. Every lane booking includes four complimentary pizzas from Westside Bistreaux.

Two kitchens, one roof

Pizza, po' boys & pho.

Westside Bistreaux handles the comfort side — fresh-baked pizzas, fried tenders, fish, jambalaya, burgers and catering trays for parties. Kim's Noodle House brings authentic Vietnamese to the lanes: pho, bánh mì, fresh spring rolls and stir-fry.
